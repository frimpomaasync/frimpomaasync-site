The 5-minute text-back script · copy it
"Hi — this is [your name] from [your business]. Sorry I missed you, I was on a job. Are you calling about [your main service]? I have [today 4:30] or [tomorrow 9 AM] — which works better?"
Text it to yourself

Send it within 5 minutes of any missed call. Two time options, one question. That's the whole trick — you answer first and make booking a one-word reply.
